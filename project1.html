ABOUT THIS GAME

Project 1 is an atmospheric single-player exploration and puzzle adventure set in a mysterious underground environment. Players must explore forgotten spaces, solve environmental puzzles, and carefully navigate through the unknown while avoiding hidden dangers.


The experience focuses on tension, discovery, and environmental storytelling, where the world itself reveals fragments of a deeper mystery. As players progress, they begin to uncover traces of a lost past buried beneath the surface.


Every discovery raises new questions — and not everything hidden underground was meant to be found.


“Some places should remain buried”
